About MyDCACalc

MyDCACalc is a free, no-nonsense dollar-cost averaging calculator built for everyday investors in stocks and crypto. No sign-up required. No data collected. Just the math.

Dollar-cost averaging is one of the most reliable long-term investment strategies available to individual investors — yet most DCA calculators out there are either ugly, limited to one asset class, or buried inside platforms trying to sell you something. We built MyDCACalc to be the tool we wished existed.

What you can do here

Use the forward projection mode to model what your portfolio could look like over time given a consistent investment schedule. Use the historical backtest mode to see how a DCA strategy would have performed against real historical averages for the S&P 500, Nasdaq, and Bitcoin.

Both stocks and crypto are supported in a single tool — because most real investors hold both.

MyDCACalc is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. All projections are illustrative estimates based on simplified compound growth models. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
