## Description (250-300 words)

Most DCA calculators do one thing — stocks or crypto, never both. Most position size calculators are buried inside paid platforms. MyDCACalc puts three free tools in one place, with no account required and nothing stored.

**What's included:**

**DCA Calculator** — Model your dollar cost averaging strategy two ways. Forward projection lets you set your own expected return and see what consistent monthly investing grows to over time. Historical backtest mode runs the numbers against real historical averages for the S&P 500, Nasdaq, and Bitcoin — so you can see what the strategy actually produced, not just what you hope it will.

**Position Size Calculator** — The tool most traders need but rarely use correctly. Enter your account size, risk percentage, entry price, and stop loss — it calculates exactly how many shares or coins to buy, your maximum loss if stopped out, and your risk/reward ratio. Works for stocks, crypto, and forex.

**Compound Interest Calculator** — Shows how money grows year by year with a stacked chart, inflation adjustment, and automatic milestones for when your balance hits $50k, $100k, $500k, and $1M.

All three calculators run entirely in your browser. No data is collected, no account is required, and there's nothing to install.

The site also includes plain-English investing guides on DCA, compound interest, lump sum vs DCA, and position sizing — all fact-checked and written for real investors, not finance professionals.

Built by an investor who got frustrated with tools that either cost money, required sign-up, or only handled one asset class.
